Welcome to the Evolution of Television

Since the early 20th century, television has revolutionized how we consume information, entertainment, and culture. From mechanical scanning systems to today's ultra-high-definition smart TVs, the journey of television reflects broader changes in technology, society, and global communication.

Television was first demonstrated successfully in the 1920s. Over the decades, it has grown from a novelty to a necessity, shaping public opinion and reflecting cultural shifts worldwide.

The first televised image was of a ventriloquist dummy named "Stooky Bill," used by John Logie Baird in 1925.
